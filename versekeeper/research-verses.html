Take Notes and Read Commentary

Have you ever wondered what a verse means? With VerseKeeper, you can easily view commentary and take notes within the Research Verses section.

Scripture Passage:

The first tab in Research Verses is the currently selected scripture. It provides a helpful and quick reference for scriptures you are researching.

Note Taking:

VerseKeeper makes note-taking easy. You can view all the notes associated with a scripture passage by tapping on or swiping to the “Notes” tab. 

You can use the following gestures to manage your notes:

  • Adding Notes: Tap the “+” icon to create a new note.
  • Editing Notes: Tap an existing note to make changes to it.
  • Deleting Notes: Long press a note to delete it.
  • Tap “Read More” to expand the view of longer notes

Commentary:

One of my favorite websites for understanding scripture is BibleRef.com. It provides free easy to understand commentary of the Bible. 

You can view their commentary within Research Verses by swiping to or tapping on the BibleRef tab. They provide commentary on the following:

  • Verse Context: Gain insight into the meaning of the verse.
  • Section Context: Get a better understanding of the passage of scripture to which the verse belongs.
  • Chapter Context: Learn what is happening in the chapter. 

Are you viewing a scripture passage comprised of many verses? You can view the verse context for each verse by tapping the verse numbers in the top right-hand corner.

Please keep in mind that VerseKeeper is not associated with BibleRef. However, I am grateful that their ministry allows the sharing, distribution, and copying of their work for non-commercial purposes. You can read more about their terms of service on their About page.
