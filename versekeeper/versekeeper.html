Why Make VerseKeeper Free?

Well, there are a lot of good Bible memorization apps in the Play Store, but none of them offered all of the features I wanted. I love memorizing chapters and books of the Bible. So, I needed a memorization app that could create custom schedules and automatically grade my accuracy as I recited memorized verses.

I created VerseKeeper to fulfill every feature I want in a Bible memorization app. I decided to make VerseKeeper free, so there wouldn't be any financial barrier to others treasuring God's Word.
